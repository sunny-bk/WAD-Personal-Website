Case Study: Restaurant Support Hub

Many small restaurants struggle to keep up with digital expectations. Customers expect online reservations, clear contact details, and fast communication. This Problem-Solving page acts as a central hub where customers can:

  • Find key contact details in one place
  • Connect with the restaurant through social media
  • Submit booking requests, feedback, or general enquiries
  • Generate a clear email message the restaurant can respond to quickly

Design Choices

This page is structured to show how web development can solve real business problems for a local restaurant:

  • Business context first: The introduction and case study explain why the page exists, framing the technical features within a real digital transformation goal.
  • Two-column layout: On larger screens, the page separates business information (menu, contact, social links) from the interactive form, making it easier for customers to find what they need.
  • Clear contact and social integration: Contact details and social media links are grouped into dedicated cards so customers can quickly choose how to get in touch.
  • Interactive form with generated email: The form uses JavaScript to build a structured email subject and body from user inputs, showing both a preview on the page and a mailto link that opens in the user’s email app.
  • Responsive and accessible design: The layout adapts to smaller screens, labels are linked to inputs, and simple language is used, supporting a wide range of users on mobile and desktop.
