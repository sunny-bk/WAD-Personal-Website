Project 1: Restaurant Support & Booking Hub Problem-Solving Page for a local restaurant CTO / UX & Front-End Designer · Project Role
Role
CTO and UX & Front-End Designer (project role), responsible for planning, designing, and building a single-page support and booking hub that acts as the restaurant’s digital front desk.
Problem
The restaurant relied on scattered channels such as phone calls and social messages for enquiries and bookings. Customers could not easily see opening hours, contact options, or how to request a table in one clear place, which risked missed messages and lost bookings.
Outcome
Designed and built an app-style support and booking hub that:
  • features a branded banner with rating and opening hours for quick reassurance,
  • includes cards for Visit & Contact, Popular Dishes, and Follow Riverside,
  • provides a guided enquiry and booking form with optional booking details,
  • uses JavaScript to generate a structured email subject and body from user inputs,
  • creates a mailto link so customers can open the message directly in their email app.
The hub gives customers one clear place to book, ask questions, and connect with the restaurant, while giving staff consistent, well-structured enquiries to respond to.

Design concept

The page is intentionally designed to feel like a modern restaurant app: a dark, atmospheric theme, a hero banner with key details, and modular cards for contact, menu highlights, and social links. The layout keeps the booking form central while still working well on mobile screens, turning a previously fragmented experience into a single, focused support hub.

Project 2: E-Commerce Brand & Digital Launch Work placement – full digital presence build Digital Solutions Assistant · Work Placement
Role
Digital Solutions Assistant, contributing to the design, build, and optimisation of a complete digital presence for a mock e-commerce brand. Focused on web development, SEO, and digital strategy.
Problem
The company needed a functional online presence but had no brand identity, no market insight, and no website capable of ranking for relevant search terms. Without these foundations it would struggle to attract traffic or convert users.
Outcome
Delivered a complete digital launch package, including:
  • designing a professional brand identity using Adobe Photoshop,
  • building an SEO-optimised e-commerce website,
  • conducting keyword and competitor research to target niche opportunities,
  • achieving first-page visibility for chosen search terms,
  • writing content and blog posts to boost organic traffic,
  • identifying high-authority backlink opportunities to improve domain trust.
This strengthened the brand’s credibility and created a sustainable flow of organic traffic.
Project 3: VOIP International Calling System Custom calling platform for a growing local business Technical Developer · Independent Project
Role
Technical Developer responsible for sourcing a VOIP provider, integrating their API, and building a simple front-end interface for international calling.
Problem
A small business relied on costly international calls for client communication. Their existing phone system was unreliable, expensive, and slowed down their ability to scale into new markets.
Outcome
Delivered a lightweight, cost-effective VOIP solution by:
  • researching and onboarding a reliable VOIP provider,
  • integrating their API into a simple web interface built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript,
  • allowing staff to make international calls securely through a browser,
  • reducing communication costs and improving global accessibility,
  • giving the business a scalable communication tool for future expansion.
The system removed a major operational bottleneck and significantly improved communication speed and reliability.
